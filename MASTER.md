# Orb

**A personal runtime that continuously learns, reasons and acts alongside its user.**

---

# Vision

Orb is a long-lived personal intelligence that grows with its user over years rather than conversations.

It continuously transforms observations into knowledge, knowledge into understanding, understanding into decisions, and decisions into meaningful action while preserving the complete history of how every conclusion was reached.

Its intelligence comes from continuity rather than any single model.

Its foundation is evidence.

Its architecture is local-first, model-independent and built to evolve for decades.

---

# Design Principles

## 1. Local First

Every capability begins on the user's devices.

Data remains under the user's control.

Synchronization extends the runtime across devices without changing ownership.

---

## 2. Device Native

Each device is a complete runtime.

The Mac and Pixel operate independently while sharing a common memory through encrypted replication.

---

## 3. Event First

Everything begins as an immutable event.

The system derives its understanding from history instead of modifying history.

Time is a first-class dimension of the architecture.

---

## 4. Evidence First

Every conclusion is traceable.

Every belief has supporting evidence.

Every decision can be explained.

Knowledge is always grounded in provenance.

---

## 5. Model Independent

Reasoning engines are interchangeable.

Models contribute intelligence, but never define the architecture.

Orb evolves as the model ecosystem evolves.

---

## 6. Continuous Understanding

Memory is continuously refined.

Understanding is continuously recomputed.

The system improves as new evidence arrives without losing the history that produced earlier conclusions.

---

## 7. Human Agency

Orb exists to extend human judgment.

It provides context, recommendations and execution while preserving meaningful human control over important decisions.
